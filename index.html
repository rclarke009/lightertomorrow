FAQ

Do I have to track calories or weigh myself?

Nope. Lighter Tomorrow focuses on tiny choices, not numbers. If you want to track elsewhere, that’s up to you.

What if I miss a day?

You’re human. The app meets you with compassion—pick a small swap tonight and begin again tomorrow.

Is this for specific diets?

No. It’s diet‑agnostic and compatible with your preferences or restrictions.

Data Types Collected

Health & Fitness

User-entered goals, reflections, and habits

(e.g., Night Prep checklist, Morning Focus "why," swap choice, End-of-Day notes)

Audio (Optional)

Voice notes if the user chooses to record.

By default, audio is not saved — only on-device transcription. Users can enable "Keep Audio" in Settings.

Identifiers

Device ID for iCloud sync only.

No advertising IDs or third-party trackers.

Data Linked to the User

Yes, if iCloud Sync is enabled: Daily entries and settings sync to the user's private iCloud account.

No, if iCloud Sync is off: All data remains on the device only.

Data Not Collected

• No contact info (name, email, phone)

• No location data

• No browsing history

• No purchase or financial info

• No third-party tracking

Data Use

App Functionality – Daily habit tracking, reminders, coach responses.

Analytics – None collected.

Developer Advertising or Marketing – None.

Third-Party Access

None.

The app does not share data with advertisers, analytics platforms, or other third parties.

Storage & Security

• All entries are stored locally on the device by default.

• iCloud sync (if enabled) uses Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure.

• Users can delete their entries or reset achievements at any time in Settings.
